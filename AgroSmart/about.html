About AgroSmart

AgroSmart is a platform designed to connect farmers with laborers, provide organic and pesticide-free farming knowledge, and give localized weather updates.

Our mission is to empower the agricultural community with easy access to resources, education, and support to ensure sustainable and profitable farming practices.

Key features of the AgroSmart platform include:

  • Real-time weather forecasting.
  • Training modules for organic farming.
  • Government scheme eligibility checks.
  • A marketplace for farming essentials.
